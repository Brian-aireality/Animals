Turtles

Here are some fun facts about turtles that we got from the National Geographic Kids website:

  • Turtles belong to one of the oldest reptile groups in the world – beating snakes, crocodiles and alligators!
  • These creatures date back to the time of the dinosaurs, over 200 million years ago – woah!

Snakes

Here are some fun facts about snakes that we got from the Science Kids website:

  • Snakes are carnivores (meat eaters).
  • Snakes have flexible jaws which allow them to eat prey bigger than their head!

Insects

Here are some fun facts about insects that we got from the Science Kids website:

  • Insects have three pairs of legs.
  • Bees, termites and ants live in well organized social colonies.
